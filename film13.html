Game Night
(2018)

In the center of the plot are Max and Annie, who are united by their love of intellectual games. Our heroes met at a competition of experts, and since then every week they gather with friends to have fun and play the so-called "night games". Once Brooks, the successful and arrogant brother of Max, whom they have not seen for about a year, invites them to play with friends in an exciting detective quest where the winner will get a luxury car. The rules are simple: one of the participants is kidnapped by villains played by professional actors, after which the rest will need to find a "hostage" using special tips in the form of riddles.
