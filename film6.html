The last of the Mohicans
(1992)

The events of the film "The Last of the Mohicans" take place in 1757, during the war between England and France for the colonial territories of North America. Colonel Monroe is besieged by the French army at the fort, to which the colonel's daughters are to arrive. They do not even suspect about the siege of their father, and do not know what danger their lives are exposed to. The girls find themselves involved in the war, but a small detachment, consisting of two Indians and a white youth, who has lived among the local residents all his life, and therefore bears the name Hawkeye, comes to their aid.
