Fury
(2014)

The motion picture takes place in April 1945. The allies are penetrating deeper into the enemy's territory, because the victory over the Germans is very close. Among the belligerents and the crew of the tank, which can be immediately distinguished from all the others by the inscription "Fury" on the muzzle. The enemy is almost defeated, but danger can lie in wait anywhere. The commander is responsible for the life of the crew members and has only one goal - to stay alive. A new recruit appears in the team, who has never fought and cannot kill quickly. With a new crew member it will become a little more difficult, the more they have a dangerous mission ahead of them.
