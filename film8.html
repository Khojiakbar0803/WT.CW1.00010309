Joker
(2019)

Events unfold in the early 1980s around the unlucky stand-up comedian Arthur Fleck, who lives with his sick mother in a small apartment in Gotham City and suffers from a rare mental illness that causes him to have fits of uncontrollable laughter. When the host of the popular late-night talk show, Murray Franklin, publicly mocks Fleck on television and his mother ends up in the hospital, he no longer finds the strength to amuse the audience with his performances. Having applied an ugly make-up to his face and taking the pseudonym Joker, the man sets a new goal for himself: to make everyone around him suffer ...
