Ono 2

In October 1988, in the small town of Derry, seven teenagers were able to overcome their fears and triumph in a mortal battle with a powerful demonic entity, who took the guise of the Dancing Clown Pennywise. After 27 years, the main characters learn with horror that the ancient evil has not disappeared anywhere, but only lurked for a while and has now awakened again. As adults, friends decide to fulfill their vows made in the past and make every effort to not only repulse the sinister merry fellow, but also put an end to him once and for all ...
