Haunted by horrific experiences from the past, Adelaide becomes increasingly worried that something bad is about to happen. Soon, her worst fears become reality: one night, they are suddenly attacked by four creepy masked strangers. But the family is really horrified when they manage to find out that each of the attackers actually looks exactly like the Wilsons themselves ...
