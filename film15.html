Jahon Vorhees
(2015)

The chilling, dynamic horror film manifesto introduces good old friends Jason Voorhees and Michael Myers. We are given an exceptional opportunity to witness an epic meeting between two of the world's most famous psychos. The narrative of a tense short film takes us to a deep forest on the outskirts of the world. A girl who has traveled many miles before finding herself at least some corner falls into a small house forgotten by God. The poor thing is obviously very scared and needs help. This is the only dwelling in the area, however, the young woman has great hopes that there is someone alive, or at least a telephone, so that she can call for help.
